Q&A

How do I know if I have received a scam email or phone call?

You may receive an unsolicited phone call (from someone claiming to be from the Bank) requesting personal or financial information, or to participate in a bank survey. You may receive an e-mail advising that you have won or inherited a large amount of money (usually from overseas) and that you need to pay a fee to release the money from the Bank.

What should I do if I got scammed?

Immediately stop contacting the scammer and report it.

Can I get my money back after I have been scammed.

Unfortunately, it is very difficult to do as scammers are difficult to track. The best thing you can do is avoid providing information to scammers in the future.

How can I prevent being scammed in the future?

You should avoid providing information to scammers, or ignore them completely. If you are unsure whether a message is a scam or not, you can use our scam detector tool.
